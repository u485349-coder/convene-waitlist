Why Convene

Less swipe fatigue. More real-world momentum.

Most dating apps reward availability theater: endless browsing, low-stakes messaging, and a lot of energy spent in talking-stage purgatory.

That leaves people stuck between swipe fatigue, ghosting, and vague "we should hang sometime" momentum that never becomes a real plan.

Convene is built for people who actually want to meet. It helps nearby active people discover each other, coordinate clearly, and earn stronger visibility by respecting other people's time.
